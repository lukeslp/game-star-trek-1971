Design Philosophy

Each variant represents a different approach to modern interface design in 2025:

üî≤ Brutalist

Inspired by brutalist architecture and early computing. Rejects decoration for pure function. Everything you need, nothing you don't. Form follows function to the extreme.

‚óØ Neumorphic

Soft UI trend popularized in 2020s. Creates depth through shadows rather than borders. Feels tactile and physical. Calm, approachable, easy on the eyes.

‚óà Glassomorphism

macOS Big Sur influence. Frosted glass with backdrop blur. Futuristic and clean. Layers create depth, transparency creates lightness.

üì± Mobile-First

iOS/Android native app conventions. Bottom nav, modal sheets, gesture controls. Optimized for one-handed use. Haptic feedback for tactile response.

‚ö° Cyberpunk

1980s sci-fi meets modern web. Neon-on-black, CRT effects, glitch aesthetic. Maximum atmosphere and personality. High contrast for drama.
