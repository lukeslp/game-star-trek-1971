# Star Trek 1971 Sound Effects

This directory contains sound effects for the Star Trek 1971 game.

## Required Sound Files

Place the following sound files in this directory:

1. **phaser.mp3** - Phaser weapon firing sound (0.3-0.5s duration)
   - Suggested: Laser/energy weapon sound
   - Free source: https://freesound.org/ (search "laser" or "sci-fi weapon")

2. **torpedo.mp3** - Torpedo launch and explosion (1-1.5s duration)
   - Suggested: Whoosh followed by explosion
   - Free source: https://freesound.org/ (search "missile explosion")

3. **explosion.mp3** - Klingon ship destruction (0.8-1s duration)
   - Suggested: Large explosion sound
   - Free source: https://freesound.org/ (search "explosion")

4. **warp.mp3** - Warp drive activation (1-2s duration)
   - Suggested: Engine hum or sci-fi drive sound
   - Free source: https://freesound.org/ (search "spaceship engine")

5. **shield.mp3** - Shield energy transfer (0.5s duration)
   - Suggested: Force field or energy shield sound
   - Free source: https://freesound.org/ (search "force field")

6. **damage.mp3** - Damage received alarm (0.5s duration)
   - Suggested: Alert beep or alarm sound
   - Free source: https://freesound.org/ (search "alarm beep")

7. **dock.mp3** - Docking at starbase (1s duration)
   - Suggested: Mechanical clunk or docking sound
   - Free source: https://freesound.org/ (search "airlock" or "dock")

8. **victory.mp3** - Mission complete fanfare (2-3s duration)
   - Suggested: Triumphant fanfare
   - Free source: https://freesound.org/ (search "victory fanfare")

9. **defeat.mp3** - Game over sound (1-2s duration)
   - Suggested: Sad trombone or defeat sound
   - Free source: https://incompetech.com/ or freesound.org

10. **alert.mp3** - Low energy/critical warning (0.2-0.3s duration)
    - Suggested: Short beep sound
    - Free source: https://freesound.org/ (search "beep")

## Sound Resources

### Free Sound Effect Libraries
- **Freesound**: https://freesound.org/ (CC-licensed sounds)
- **Kenney**: https://kenney.nl/assets (game audio assets)
- **Zapsplat**: https://www.zapsplat.com/ (free tier available)
- **SoundBible**: https://soundbible.com/ (public domain sounds)

### Recommended Search Terms
- Sci-fi sound effects
- Star Trek sound effects
- Retro game sounds
- 8-bit sound effects (for authentic 1971 feel)

## Audio Format Guidelines

- **Format**: MP3 (best browser compatibility)
- **Sample Rate**: 44.1 kHz
- **Bit Rate**: 128 kbps (good quality, small file size)
- **Channels**: Mono or Stereo
- **Total Size**: Keep each file under 100KB for fast loading

## Testing

After adding sound files, the game will automatically load them. Use the sound toggle button in the game header to enable/disable sounds.

## Fallback Behavior

If sound files are missing, the game will continue to work normally without audio. The sound manager handles missing files gracefully with console warnings only.
